How to apply for your mortgage


You can continue and complete your mortgage application by choosing from one of the options below.


Recommended for first time buyers

Continue your application with advice

One of our Mortgage Advisers will call you to confirm details and book in your telephone mortgage appointment.

 

Are you sure you'd like to continue with a phone advisor?

If you continue by phone, you will not be offered our online exclusive products as these are only available if you complete your full application online.

If you chose to continue by phone your online application will be finished and you will not be able to return to it.

Continue your application without advice

Choose your product and complete your application.

Important information

YOUR HOME MAY BE REPOSSESSED IF YOU DO NOT KEEP UP REPAYMENTS ON YOUR MORTGAGE

All applications are subject to status and our lending criteria. This means that the amount we will lend you will depend on your individual circumstances, the type of property and the amount you borrow. For example, we may require a higher deposit if you are buying a flat or a new build property.
